Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my services

How do I book a session with you?
+
Simply click any "Book" button on this website to visit my Topmate profile. Choose the service you need, select a convenient time slot, and complete the booking. You'll receive an instant confirmation email with all session details and meeting link.
What can I expect from a career coaching session?
+
Each session is tailored to your specific needs. We'll discuss your career goals, current challenges, and create a personalized action plan. You'll receive practical strategies, valuable resources, and ongoing accountability to help you achieve your objectives quickly and effectively.
How long does it take to see results?
+
Results vary based on your goals and commitment. Many clients see improvements in their job search within 2-4 weeks after resume and LinkedIn optimizations. Career transitions typically take 3-6 months with consistent effort. I provide ongoing support to ensure you achieve your goals. Please note this does not guarantee a job offer.
Do you work with clients outside India?
+
Absolutely! All my sessions are conducted virtually via video call, so I work with clients worldwide. I accommodate different time zones and have successfully helped professionals from Europe, USA, UK, Canada, UAE, Singapore, and many other countries achieve their career goals.
What if I'm not sure which service I need?
+
No problem at all! You can book a discovery consultation call where we'll discuss your situation in detail. Based on our conversation, I'll recommend the best approach for your specific needs and budget. Feel free to reach out on LinkedIn or Instagram with questions before booking.
