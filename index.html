Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

How long does the career coaching process take? +

The duration varies based on your goals. On average, clients see results within 1-3 months. Some land jobs faster, while career transitions may take longer. I provide ongoing support throughout your journey.

Do you offer services for people transitioning careers? +

Absolutely! Career transitions are one of my specialties. I'll help you identify transferable skills, rebrand your profile, and strategically position yourself for success in your new field.

What makes your approach different? +

I combine my real-world project management experience with personalized coaching. Unlike generic career advice, I tailor strategies to your unique situation, industry, and goals. You're not just a client ‚Äì you're a partner in your success.

Can you help me if I'm already employed but looking to advance? +

Yes! I work with professionals at all career stages. Whether you're seeking promotion, better compensation, or a more fulfilling role, I'll help you strategize, build your brand, and make your next career move.

Do you offer packages or one-time services? +

Both! I offer comprehensive coaching packages and individual services like resume reviews, LinkedIn optimization, or mock interviews. We can discuss what works best for your needs and budget during our discovery call.
